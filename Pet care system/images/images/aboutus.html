Family Pet Care was established in July 2010 aiming to provide better and professional service for all the pet owners . Our services include treatments & vaccination, surgeries , grooming , dental care , pet boarding & hospitalization, pet taxi & mobile veterinary service, Laboratory facilities, Digital X-ray, Scanning etc.


We also provide quality products for your little friends and accept donations in order to help the stray animals. Our services are available in and around Colombo


Our Vision


To be the leading professional and quality veterinary care provider in Sri Lanka



Our Mission


To assist you and your four legged friends with love, care and respect. At Family Pet Care We are dedicated and bound to provide the best service for all your pet's needs
